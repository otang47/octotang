Culture

Dan dan noodles are a classic street food from Szechuan, China. Legend has it that there was a hawker who would carry a pole (dan) on his shoulders up and down the streets of Chengdu. Attached to each ends were baskets, one containing handmade noodles and the other containing the Dan Dan sauce.

Legend also has it that if you look behind the dan dan man there is a starving college student following the sound of his sales cries. That's me.
